Roofing Maintenance Tips

Proper maintenance is key to extending the life of your roof and preventing costly repairs. Follow these expert tips to keep your roof in top condition year-round.
Regular Inspections

Schedule bi-annual roof inspections to catch and address minor issues before they become major problems.

Clean Gutters

Regularly clean gutters and downspouts to prevent water buildup and potential damage to your roof and foundation.

Trim Overhanging Branches

Keep tree branches away from your roof to prevent damage from falling limbs and reduce debris accumulation.

Remove Snow and Ice

In winter, safely remove excess snow and ice from your roof to prevent damage from the added weight and ice dams.

Address Moss and Algae

Clean any moss or algae growth to prevent moisture retention and potential shingle damage.

Professional Maintenance

Schedule professional maintenance as recommended by your roofing contractor to ensure longevity and optimal performance.

Check for Leaks

Regularly inspect your attic and ceilings for signs of water stains or leaks, especially after heavy rainfall.

Ensure Proper Ventilation

Maintain good attic ventilation to prevent heat and moisture buildup, which can damage your roof from the inside.

Check Flashing

Inspect and maintain flashing around chimneys, vents, and other roof penetrations to prevent water intrusion.
